About Us

In 2023, Pawon Java was established as a form of dedication and appreciation to the diverse people of Indonesia, especially the island of Java, to help elevate and introduce tradition and culture through its culinary, and to make it a mainstay menu.

Pawon Java is an online restaurant that sells a variety of Javanese food and beverage menus in large portions for various celebrations such as weddings and other celebrations that require large portions of food.

Forget about the daily stress and come relax with us. After tasting any of our menu items, u will never want to leave!
